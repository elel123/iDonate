Whole Blood Donation

  • Time Commitment - Usually one hour (10 - 15 minutes for actual donation)
  • Procedure Description - the standard blood donation that takes about one unit
    of plasma, red blood cells, platelets, and other antibodies
  • Preferred Blood Type - Open to everyone
  • Required Wait Time Before Next Donation - 56 days
  • 1 pint saves 3 lives, especially surgery patients and trauma victims!

    Power Red

    • Requirements - Males must be at least 17 years old, 5’1”, and 130 lbs. Females
      must be at least 19 years old, 5’5”, and 150 lbs.
    • Time Commitment - Usually an hour and a half (30 - 45 minutes for actual donation)
    • Procedure Description - utilizes a special machine to extract two units of red
      blood cells while returning platelets and plasma to your bloodstream. Usually feels hydrating.
    • Preferred Blood Type - A-, B-, O-, O+
    • Required Wait Time Before Next Donation - 112 days
    • Twice as Nice! - Saves 6 lives, especially helping trauma patients and premature babies.
