Welcome to iDonate


Purpose

iDonate streamlines blood donation to increase the fluidity of donation day and encourage more people to donate.

Why should YOU donate?

  • More than 25% of Americans will need blood, or know someone who will need blood, at least once
    in their life. Giving blood now ensures your access to it in the future. Give back to the system.
  • Be a LIVES saver! Every pint of blood saves 3 lives in the future! Premature babies, burn patients,
    trauma victims, and cancer diagnosed children DEPEND on donations from people like you!
  • Periodic blood donations reduce iron storages, which severely reduce chances of getting genetic
    diseases such as hemochromatosis and heart attacks by 88%!
  • Diminishes your chances of cardiovascular diseases and bone marrow disease, as well as lower your
    risk of cancer and early mortality.
